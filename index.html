Dominique Bullard (She/Her)

Dominique Bullard is a 27-year-old full-time artist based in Brooklyn, NYC, whose vibrant creativity shines through her work—even crafting her own profile picture! A lover of color, energy, and enthusiasm, Dominique is passionate about gaming (PS5 favorites include Monster Hunter Wilds, FFIV, and Marvel Rivals), often diving deep into character lore for games like Metal Gear Solid and Bloodborne. When she’s not gaming or creating art, she’s studying Japanese (adding to her knowledge of French and Spanish) and dreaming of her next adventure to Japan, inspired by her love of anime and K-pop. Dominique’s life is filled with curiosity, cats (she has three!), and a bold aesthetic—her favorite colors, red and black, reflect her dynamic personality.
